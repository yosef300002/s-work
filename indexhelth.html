Health: A state of well-being :

Health is a state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity. It is a resource for everyday life, not the objective of living. Health is a positive concept emphasizing social and personal resources, as well as physical capacities

How to Maintain Good Health :

Maintaining good health is essential for living a long and fulfilling life. It involves making healthy choices in all aspects of your life, including your diet, exercise habits, and mental well-being. While it may seem daunting to make all these changes at once, even small steps can make a big difference.
